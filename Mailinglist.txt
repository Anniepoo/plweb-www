# Mailing list

The mailing list for SWI-Prolog specific issues is a [Google
group](http://groups.google.com/). This list is used for announcements
as well as questions and discussion amoung users. On average, traffic is
5-10 messages per day. Useful info:

  - The connected forum can be viewed [here](</forum>) or at
    [google](http://groups.google.com/group/swi-prolog).

  - If you have a Google account, you can subscribe through the
    [forum](http://groups.google.com/group/swi-prolog).

  - If you have *no* Google account, you can subscribe by sending a
    mail to

    ==
    swi-prolog+subscribe@googlegroups.com
    ==

## Old mailing list

Before being moved to a Google group (May 1, 2014), the mailing list was
kindly hosted by the University of Bonn. The archives of this list are
still available
[here](<https://lists.iai.uni-bonn.de/pipermail/swi-prolog>).
